Legal Services

Legal Services for Israelis and Hebrew Speakers Abroad

Below is an overview of the main services I provide. For each matter we can agree in advance on the scope of work, price, and timeline – so there are no surprises.

Legal Translation (Non-Notarial)

Professional translation of legal documents and agreements. The focus is on meaning, legal effect, and risk – not just literal wording. Typical documents include:

• Business agreements
• Service contracts
• Lease and rental agreements
• Partnership or investment documents

Contracts & Agreements

Drafting, reviewing, and clarifying business, rental, or partnership agreements. The goal is to identify unclear clauses, hidden risks, and missing protections.

I can help you:

• Prepare new agreements suited to your situation
• Review existing drafts before you sign
• Negotiate clearer, fairer terms

Support for Entrepreneurs & Business Formation

Assisting entrepreneurs with business formation, structure, and risk evaluation in a foreign environment. We focus on:

• Choosing a basic structure with local professionals
• Understanding key obligations and liabilities
• Clarifying agreements between partners

Bilingual Document Review

Clear explanations of Spanish / English documents in Hebrew. You send the document; we go through the key clauses and their consequences, in plain Hebrew.

This is especially useful for:

• Employment agreements
• Long-term leases
• Supplier and service contracts

Immigration & Employment Support

Guidance and referrals to trusted Spanish immigration or labor attorneys, when local representation is needed. I can help you:

• Clarify your situation and goals
• Prepare relevant documents
• Connect with suitable local counsel
